Hi I'm Phoebe:

I am a developer and UX/UI designer based in Vancouver, who recently graduated at Simon Fraser University with a major in Interactive Arts and Technology (BSc.) and a minor in Print and Digital Publishing.

As a designer, I like to push the boundaries of possibility and expectation when it comes to creating innovative interactive experiences.

My greatest skills lies in being a quick learner; eager to explore new technologies and utilizing it in my projects. I love to discover how things work and why they are created. With this, I am confident in my ability to create high quality, effective outputs with attention to detail. My approach to creating innovative experiences is to always consider end-users and as a result, I produce targeted, clean and intuitive experiences that resonate with my audiences.
